Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the “App”), a real-world scavenger hunt game for trading card collectibles. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Profile photo or avatar (optional)
  • Location data (when enabled)
  • Device ID or advertising identifiers

1.2 Location Data

The App uses your device's GPS or other location technology to deliver location-based scavenger hunt experiences. We only collect location data with your consent, and you may revoke access at any time via device settings.

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect data about your interactions with the App, including:

  • App feature usage and session duration
  • Items collected, trades completed, and progress history
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

1.4 Payment and Transaction Data

If you make in-app purchases, your payment is processed through third-party app stores (e.g., Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not store your full payment details, but we may receive confirmation of transaction status and amounts.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Deliver core gameplay functionality and features
  • Personalize your experience
  • Track your in-game progress and inventory
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Enforce our terms and protect against fraud or abuse
  • Send you updates, promotions, or important service messages (you may opt out)
  • Analyze usage trends and improve app performance

3. Legal Basis for Processing (for EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Your consent
  • Contractual necessity (e.g., delivering the App's functionality)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., improving the App, preventing abuse)

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who perform functions such as:

  • Cloud storage and hosting
  • Analytics (e.g., Google Analytics for Firebase)
  • Error monitoring (e.g., Sentry or Crashlytics)
  • Email and push notification delivery

These providers are contractually obligated to keep your data secure and confidential.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent (for example, location permissions)
  • Request a copy of your stored data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at elpaso.pokecache@gmail.com.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or the age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us to have it deleted.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction, including the United States. These countries may have different data protection laws. We take steps to ensure your data is adequately protected in such cases.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or other communication methods. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

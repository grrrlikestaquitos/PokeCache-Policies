Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use our app, a real-world scavenger hunt experience for trading card collectibles (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: To enable scavenger hunts and location-based gameplay, we collect your device’s GPS location with your consent.
  • Account Information: If you sign up, we may collect a username, email address, and password.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, and app version to improve performance and fix bugs.
  • Gameplay Data: We may collect data about in-app actions such as discovered items, trades, and event participation.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Enable core features such as location-based discovery and trading
  • Improve the app experience and diagnose technical issues
  • Communicate with users about updates, features, or events
  • Ensure fairness and prevent abuse within the game

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics), but only as needed and under strict confidentiality.

4. Your Choices

You can opt out of location services by changing your device settings, but this may limit some game features. You may also request account deletion or data removal by contacting us.

5. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. Use strong passwords and be cautious with your account.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If significant changes are made, we will notify users in the app or via email.
